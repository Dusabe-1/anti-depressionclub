ADHCI ☰

Who we are?


Name of the Club: Ant-depression and healing circle initiative club

Mission Statement: to provide a safe space for individuals dealing with depression and emotional challenges to heal and grow.

Vision Statement: creating a community where mental health is prioritized and individuals feel empowered).


Core Goals:


1.Supporting mental health awareness.

2.Providing peer support through healing circles.

3.Educating the community on coping mechanisms and resilience.

4.Collaborating with mental health professionals and organizations



Services and Activities


Regular healing circle meetings.

Workshops on stress management, mindfulness, or emotional well-being.

Partnerships with therapists or counselors.

Crisis helpline services or referrals.

Target Audience


Individuals struggling with depression or stress.

Family and friends seeking to support loved ones.

Community members interested in mental health advocacy.

Core Values/principles that guide club


✓ Empathy and compassion.

✓ Inclusivity and non-judgment.

✓ Privacy and confidentiality.

✓ Community-driven support.
